# MediCare-AI
MediCare AI is a smart healthcare assistant that analyzes user health data, symptoms, lifestyle, and medical reports to provide AI-powered insights, predictions, and personalized health recommendations for better well-being.
🏥 MediCare AI
Your Personal AI Healthcare Assistant
📖 Overview

MediCare AI is an AI-powered healthcare assistant designed to help users monitor and understand their health. By analyzing vitals, lifestyle habits, symptoms, and medical reports, the system provides intelligent insights, risk predictions, and personalized health suggestions.

🚀 Key Features
🧑‍⚕️ Health Profile Management
Input age, lifestyle, and daily routines
Maintain complete personal health records
❤️ Vitals Monitoring
Heart Rate (BPM)
Blood Pressure
Blood Sugar Levels
Real-time health insights
🧠 AI Health Analysis
Detects possible health risks
Provides smart recommendations
Predicts potential conditions based on data
🥗 Lifestyle & Diet Analysis
Analyzes daily routines and food habits
Suggests healthier lifestyle improvements
📄 Medical File Upload
Upload prescriptions/reports (PNG, JPG, GIF)
Extracts and analyzes medical information
💊 Disease & Symptom Tracking
Input current diseases or symptoms
AI suggests possible precautions and care
